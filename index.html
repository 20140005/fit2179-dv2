Can Students Still Afford Australian Cities?

Rising rents, increasing living expenses, and growing financial pressure are reshaping student life across Australia.

Sydney students now require over 65 work hours weekly to afford average living costs.

Average weekly work hours needed for Sydney students to cover living costs

Where Is Student Life Most Expensive?

Living costs vary significantly across Australian cities. While Sydney remains the most expensive city for students, smaller cities such as Hobart continue to offer comparatively affordable lifestyles.

Sydney Remains Australia's Least Affordable Student City

Students in Sydney face the highest weekly living costs in Australia, driven primarily by rapidly increasing rental prices.

The Rent Crisis

Since 2020, rental prices across Australia have increased sharply, creating significant financial pressure for university students.

Rent Growth Has Accelerated Since 2020

Rental prices across major Australian cities have increased dramatically over the last five years, placing increasing pressure on students.

Where Does Student Money Go?

Rent consumes the largest portion of student budgets, leaving limited flexibility for food, transport, utilities, and entertainment.

The Human Impact

Students increasingly rely on part-time work to manage rising living costs, with some cities requiring unsustainable working hours.

Students Must Work Longer Hours Than Ever Before

In some Australian cities, students now require over 60 hours of work per week to cover average living expenses.

Changing Spending Patterns

Over time, essential expenses such as rent and utilities have consumed increasingly larger portions of student budgets.

Conclusion

While Australian cities continue attracting university students, rising living costs are making student life increasingly difficult and financially unsustainable.

The Cost of Education Is No Longer Just Tuition

For many students, rising living costs are becoming one of the greatest barriers to higher education in Australia.
